Workshop Facilitators

Ryan Bates
Owner & Producer
Railscasts
Ryan helps thousands of developers learn Ruby on Rails, as well as javascript frameworks and all things Rails-related, on his self-produced screencast site, Railscasts. Ryan is excited to bring his teaching skills into a live in-person workshop for the first time ever!
The ZEAL Team
Rob, Adam, Trever & Sean
Zeal
ZEAL is a dev studio built on a passion for Rails, TDD, pair programming, and the Agile process. Zeal's desire to spread this knowledge and passion inspired the creation of the Rogue Rails workshop and conference.

Sarah Mei
